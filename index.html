الحَمْدُ والثَّنَاءPraise & Glorification of Allah

The Prophet ﷺ taught: "When one of you prays, let him begin by glorifying his Lord and praising Him, then send ṣalawāt upon the Prophet, then ask for whatever he wishes." Open the door of duʿāʾ with abundant ḥamd.
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ، حَمْدًا كَثِيرًا طَيِّبًا مُبَارَكًا فِيهِ، مِلْءَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمِلْءَ الْأَرْضِ، وَمِلْءَ مَا بَيْنَهُمَا، وَمِلْءَ مَا شِئْتَ مِنْ شَيْءٍ بَعْدُ، أَهْلَ الثَّنَاءِ وَالْمَجْدِ.
All praise is for Allah, Lord of all the worlds — praise abundant, pure, and blessed, filling the heavens and the earth, all that is between them, and whatever else You will, O Worthy of all praise and majesty.
Ṣaḥīḥ Muslim
اللَّهُمَّ لَكَ الْحَمْدُ، أَنْتَ قَيِّمُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ وَمَنْ فِيهِنَّ، وَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ، لَكَ مُلْكُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ وَمَنْ فِيهِنَّ، وَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ، أَنْتَ نُورُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ وَمَنْ فِيهِنَّ، وَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ، أَنْتَ الْحَقُّ، وَوَعْدُكَ الْحَقُّ، وَلِقَاؤُكَ حَقٌّ، وَقَوْلُكَ حَقٌّ، وَالْجَنَّةُ حَقٌّ، وَالنَّارُ حَقٌّ، وَالنَّبِيُّونَ حَقٌّ، وَمُحَمَّدٌ ﷺ حَقٌّ، وَالسَّاعَةُ حَقٌّ. اللَّهُمَّ لَكَ أَسْلَمْتُ، وَبِكَ آمَنْتُ، وَعَلَيْكَ تَوَكَّلْتُ، وَإِلَيْكَ أَنَبْتُ، وَبِكَ خَاصَمْتُ، وَإِلَيْكَ حَاكَمْتُ، فَاغْفِرْ لِي مَا قَدَّمْتُ وَمَا أَخَّرْتُ، وَمَا أَسْرَرْتُ وَمَا أَعْلَنْتُاللَّهُمَّ لَكَ أَسْلَمْنَا، وَبِكَ آمَنَّا، وَعَلَيْكَ تَوَكَّلْنَا، وَإِلَيْكَ أَنَبْنَا، وَبِكَ خَاصَمْنَا، وَإِلَيْكَ حَاكَمْنَا، فَاغْفِرْ لَنَا مَا قَدَّمْنَا وَمَا أَخَّرْنَا، وَمَا أَسْرَرْنَا وَمَا أَعْلَنَّا، أَنْتَ الْمُقَدِّمُ وَأَنْتَ الْمُؤَخِّرُ، لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ.

الصَّلَاةُ عَلَى النَّبِيِّ ﷺBlessings upon the Prophet

After ḥamd, send ṣalawāt — a station of acceptance, for duʿāʾ is suspended until ṣalawāt is sent. Send it at the beginning, throughout, and at the end.
اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَعَلَى آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ، كَمَا صَلَّيْتَ عَلَى إِبْرَاهِيمَ وَعَلَى آلِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ، إِنَّكَ حَمِيدٌ مَجِيدٌ. اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَعَلَى آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ، كَمَا بَارَكْتَ عَلَى إِبْرَاهِيمَ وَعَلَى آلِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ، إِنَّكَ حَمِيدٌ مَجِيدٌ.
O Allah, send Your blessings upon Muḥammad and the family of Muḥammad, as You blessed Ibrāhīm and the family of Ibrāhīm; indeed You are Praiseworthy, Most Glorious. O Allah, bestow Your favour upon Muḥammad and the family of Muḥammad, as You favoured Ibrāhīm and the family of Ibrāhīm; indeed You are Praiseworthy, Most Glorious.
Ṣaḥīḥ al-Bukhārī & Muslim
اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ وَسَلِّمْ وَبَارِكْ عَلَى نَبِيِّنَا مُحَمَّدٍ، عَدَدَ خَلْقِكَ، وَرِضَا نَفْسِكَ، وَزِنَةَ عَرْشِكَ، وَمِدَادَ كَلِمَاتِكَ.
O Allah, send blessings, peace and favour upon our Prophet Muḥammad — as many as the number of Your creation, as pleases You, as weighty as Your Throne, and as endless as the ink of Your words.

التَّهْلِيلُ — أَفْضَلُ ذِكْرِ عَرَفَةThe Best Word of Arafah

The very word the Prophet ﷺ named the best of what he and all the Prophets said on this day. Linger here — repeat it slowly, with the heart, many times.
لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ، لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ، وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ.
There is no god but Allah alone, with no partner. His is the dominion, and His is all praise, and He is able to do all things.

القُرْآنُ والعِلْمُ والنُّورThe Qur'an, Knowledge & Light

The Prophet ﷺ said the People of the Qur'an are the people of Allah and His elect. Ask to be made one of them — to recite it, carry it in your breast, understand it, and be lit by it.
اللَّهُمَّ ارْحَمْنَا بِالْقُرْآنِ، وَاجْعَلْهُ لَنَا إِمَامًا وَنُورًا وَهُدًى وَرَحْمَةً. اللَّهُمَّ ذَكِّرْنَا مِنْهُ مَا نَسِينَا، وَعَلِّمْنَا مِنْهُ مَا جَهِلْنَا، وَارْزُقْنَا تِلَاوَتَهُ آنَاءَ اللَّيْلِ وَأَطْرَافَ النَّهَارِ عَلَى الْوَجْهِ الَّذِي يُرْضِيكَ عَنَّا، وَاجْعَلْهُ لَنَا حُجَّةً يَا رَبَّ الْعَالَمِينَ.
O Allah, have mercy on us through the Qur'an; make it for us a leader, a light, a guidance, and a mercy. O Allah, remind us of what we have forgotten of it, teach us what we are ignorant of, grant us its recitation through the hours of the night and the ends of the day in the manner that pleases You, and make it a proof for us, O Lord of all the worlds.

طَلَبُ الخِيَرَةِ وحُسْنُ الِاخْتِيَارAsking for the Best Choice

Ask Allah — who knows what you do not — to choose for you, and to make you pleased with what He chooses. This is the prophetic istikhārah, said over every matter before you.
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْتَخِيرُكَ بِعِلْمِكَ، وَأَسْتَقْدِرُكَ بِقُدْرَتِكَ، وَأَسْأَلُكَ مِنْ فَضْلِكَ الْعَظِيمِ، فَإِنَّكَ تَقْدِرُ وَلَا أَقْدِرُ، وَتَعْلَمُ وَلَا أَعْلَمُ، وَأَنْتَ عَلَّامُ الْغُيُوبِ. اللَّهُمَّ إِنْ كُنْتَ تَعْلَمُ أَنَّ هَذَا الْأَمْرَ خَيْرٌ لِي فِي دِينِي وَمَعَاشِي وَعَاقِبَةِ أَمْرِي، فَاقْدُرْهُ لِي وَيَسِّرْهُ لِي ثُمَّ بَارِكْ لِي فِيهِ، وَإِنْ كُنْتَ تَعْلَمُ أَنَّ هَذَا الْأَمْرَ شَرٌّ لِي فِي دِينِي وَمَعَاشِي وَعَاقِبَةِ أَمْرِي، فَاصْرِفْهُ عَنِّي وَاصْرِفْنِي عَنْهُ، وَاقْدُرْ لِيَ الْخَيْرَ حَيْثُ كَانَ ثُمَّ رَضِّنِي بِهِ.

العَافِيَةُ والشِّفَاءWell-being & Healing

After certainty of faith, nothing better is given than ʿāfiyah. Ask for health — not as an end, but as strength to worship, work, and serve in His path.
اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ الشَّافِي الْمُعَافِي، لَا شِفَاءَ إِلَّا شِفَاؤُكَ، وَأَنْتَ الْقَائِلُ عَلَى لِسَانِ خَلِيلِكَ: ﴿وَإِذَا مَرِضْتُ فَهُوَ يَشْفِينِ﴾، فَأَسْأَلُكَ يَا شَافِي أَنْ تَهَبَنِي تَمَامَ الصِّحَّةِ وَدَوَامَ الْعَافِيَةِ فِي بَدَنِي وَدِينِي وَعَقْلِي، وَأَنْ تُمَتِّعَنِي بِقُوَّةٍ أَعْمَلُ بِهَا فِي طَاعَتِكَ، وَأَسْعَى بِهَا فِي سَبِيلِكَ، وَأَخْدُمُ بِهَا دِينَكَ وَعِبَادَكَ.اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ الشَّافِي الْمُعَافِي، لَا شِفَاءَ إِلَّا شِفَاؤُكَ، وَأَنْتَ الْقَائِلُ عَلَى لِسَانِ خَلِيلِكَ: ﴿وَإِذَا مَرِضْتُ فَهُوَ يَشْفِينِ﴾، فَنَسْأَلُكَ يَا شَافِي أَنْ تَهَبَنَا تَمَامَ الصِّحَّةِ وَدَوَامَ الْعَافِيَةِ فِي أَبْدَانِنَا وَدِينِنَا وَعُقُولِنَا، وَأَنْ تُمَتِّعَنَا بِقُوَّةٍ نَعْمَلُ بِهَا فِي طَاعَتِكَ، وَنَسْعَى بِهَا فِي سَبِيلِكَ، وَنَخْدُمُ بِهَا دِينَكَ وَعِبَادَكَ.
O Allah, You are the Healer, the Granter of well-being — there is no healing but Yours. You said upon the tongue of Your beloved Khalīl, "And when I am ill, it is He who cures me." So I ask You, O Healer, to grant me complete health and lasting well-being in my body, religion, and mind, and to let me enjoy strength by which I act in Your obedience, strive in Your path, and serve Your religion and servants.O Allah, You are the Healer, the Granter of well-being — there is no healing but Yours. You said upon the tongue of Your beloved Khalīl, "And when I am ill, it is He who cures me." So we ask You, O Healer, to grant us complete health and lasting well-being in our bodies, religion, and minds, and to let us enjoy strength by which we act in Your obedience, strive in Your path, and serve Your religion and servants.

O Allah, I ask You for pardon and well-being in this world and the Hereafter; in my religion, worldly life, family, and wealth. O Allah, conceal my faults, calm my fears, and guard me from before me and behind me, from my right and my left, and from above me; and I seek refuge in Your greatness from being taken unawares from beneath me.O Allah, we ask You for pardon and well-being in this world and the Hereafter; in our religion, worldly life, families, and wealth. O Allah, conceal our faults, calm our fears, and guard us from before us and behind us, from our right and our left, and from above us; and we seek refuge in Your greatness from being taken unawares from beneath us.

الحِفْظُ مِنَ العَيْنِ والسِّحْرِ والحَسَدProtection from the Evil Eye, Siḥr & Envy

The Prophet ﷺ sought refuge for himself and his grandsons with these words and these sūrahs. Recite them with conviction over yourself and those you love.
قُلْ هُوَ اللهُ أَحَدٌ ۝ اللهُ الصَّمَدُ ۝ لَمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُولَدْ ۝ وَلَمْ يَكُنْ لَهُ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ.
Say: He is Allah, the One. Allah, the Eternal Refuge. He neither begets nor was begotten, and there is none comparable to Him.
Sūrat al-Ikhlāṣ · recite 3×
قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ الْفَلَقِ ۝ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ ۝ وَمِنْ شَرِّ غَاسِقٍ إِذَا وَقَبَ ۝ وَمِنْ شَرِّ النَّفَّاثَاتِ فِي الْعُقَدِ ۝ وَمِنْ شَرِّ حَاسِدٍ إِذَا حَسَدَ.
Say: I seek refuge in the Lord of daybreak, from the evil of what He created, from the evil of darkness when it settles, from the evil of those who blow upon knots, and from the evil of an envier when he envies.
Sūrat al-Falaq · recite 3×
قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ النَّاسِ ۝ مَلِكِ النَّاسِ ۝ إِلَهِ النَّاسِ ۝ مِنْ شَرِّ الْوَسْوَاسِ الْخَنَّاسِ ۝ الَّذِي يُوَسْوِسُ فِي صُدُورِ النَّاسِ ۝ مِنَ الْجِنَّةِ وَالنَّاسِ.
Say: I seek refuge in the Lord of mankind, the Sovereign of mankind, the God of mankind, from the evil of the retreating whisperer who whispers in the breasts of mankind — from among the jinn and mankind.
Sūrat al-Nās · recite 3×
اللهُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ، لَا تَأْخُذُهُ سِنَةٌ وَلَا نَوْمٌ، لَهُ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ، مَنْ ذَا الَّذِي يَشْفَعُ عِنْدَهُ إِلَّا بِإِذْنِهِ، يَعْلَمُ مَا بَيْنَ أَيْدِيهِمْ وَمَا خَلْفَهُمْ، وَلَا يُحِيطُونَ بِشَيْءٍ مِنْ عِلْمِهِ إِلَّا بِمَا شَاءَ، وَسِعَ كُرْسِيُّهُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ، وَلَا يَئُودُهُ حِفْظُهُمَا، وَهُوَ الْعَلِيُّ الْعَظِيمُ.
Allah — there is no god but He, the Ever-Living, the Sustainer. Neither drowsiness nor sleep overtakes Him. To Him belongs all that is in the heavens and the earth. Who can intercede with Him except by His leave? He knows what lies before them and behind them, and they encompass nothing of His knowledge except what He wills. His Footstool extends over the heavens and the earth, and their preservation does not weary Him; and He is the Most High, the Magnificent.

صَلَاحُ الدُّنْيَاWorldly Affairs — Ease, Debt & Relief

Ask for everything — even the strap of your sandal, as he ﷺ taught. Allah is never burdened by what you ask.
اللَّهُمَّ لَا سَهْلَ إِلَّا مَا جَعَلْتَهُ سَهْلًا، وَأَنْتَ تَجْعَلُ الْحَزْنَ إِذَا شِئْتَ سَهْلًا. اللَّهُمَّ يَسِّرْ لِي أَمْرِي، وَفَرِّجْ هَمِّي، وَاكْشِفْ كَرْبِي.اللَّهُمَّ لَا سَهْلَ إِلَّا مَا جَعَلْتَهُ سَهْلًا، وَأَنْتَ تَجْعَلُ الْحَزْنَ إِذَا شِئْتَ سَهْلًا. اللَّهُمَّ يَسِّرْ لَنَا أُمُورَنَا، وَفَرِّجْ هُمُومَنَا، وَاكْشِفْ كُرُوبَنَا.

O Allah, make my affair easy for me, relieve my worry, and remove my distress.O Allah, there is no ease except in what You make easy, and You make hardship, if You will, easy. اللَّهُمَّ مَا سَأَلْتُكَ مِنْ خَيْرٍ فَأَعْطِنِيهِ، وَمَا لَمْ أَسْأَلْكَ مِنْ خَيْرٍ فَآتِنِيهِ، وَمَا اسْتَعَذْتُ مِنْهُ مِنْ شَرٍّ فَأَعِذْنِي مِنْهُ، وَمَا لَمْ أَسْتَعِذْكَ مِنْهُ مِنْ شَرٍّ فَقِنِي، وَمَا قَصُرَ عَنْهُ دُعَائِي مِنْ خَيْرٍ وَعَدْتَهُ أَحَدًا مِنْ خَلْقِكَ فَإِنِّي أَرْغَبُ إِلَيْكَ فِيهِ، يَا رَبَّ الْعَالَمِينَ.اللَّهُمَّ مَا سَأَلْنَاكَ مِنْ خَيْرٍ فَأَعْطِنَاهُ، وَمَا لَمْ نَسْأَلْكَ مِنْ خَيْرٍ فَآتِنَاهُ، وَمَا اسْتَعَذْنَا مِنْهُ مِنْ شَرٍّ فَأَعِذْنَا مِنْهُ، وَمَا لَمْ نَسْتَعِذْكَ مِنْهُ مِنْ شَرٍّ فَقِنَا، وَمَا قَصُرَ عَنْهُ دُعَاؤُنَا مِنْ خَيْرٍ وَعَدْتَهُ أَحَدًا مِنْ خَلْقِكَ فَإِنَّا نَرْغَبُ إِلَيْكَ فِيهِ، يَا رَبَّ الْعَالَمِينَ.
O Allah, whatever good I have asked of You, grant it to me; whatever good I did not ask, bestow it; whatever evil I sought refuge from, protect me from it; whatever evil I did not seek refuge from, guard me from it too; and whatever good my supplication fell short of — any good You promised to any of Your creation — I earnestly desire it from You, O Lord of all the worlds.O Allah, whatever good we have asked of You, grant it to us; whatever good we did not ask, bestow it; whatever evil we sought refuge from, protect us from it; whatever evil we did not seek refuge from, guard us from it too; and whatever good our supplication fell short of — any good You promised to any of Your creation — we earnestly desire it from You, O Lord of all the worlds.
اللَّهُمَّ لَا تَدَعْ لَنَا فِي هَذَا الْمَوْقِفِ الْعَظِيمِ ذَنْبًا إِلَّا غَفَرْتَهُ، وَلَا هَمًّا إِلَّا فَرَّجْتَهُ، وَلَا دَيْنًا إِلَّا قَضَيْتَهُ، وَلَا مَرِيضًا إِلَّا شَفَيْتَهُ، وَلَا مَيِّتًا إِلَّا رَحِمْتَهُ، وَلَا حَاجَةً مِنْ حَوَائِجِ الدُّنْيَا وَالْآخِرَةِ هِيَ لَكَ رِضًا وَلَنَا فِيهَا صَلَاحٌ إِلَّا قَضَيْتَهَا وَيَسَّرْتَهَا، يَا أَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِينَ.
O Allah, in this mighty standing, leave for us no sin but that You forgive it, no worry but that You relieve it, no debt but that You settle it, no sick person but that You heal them, no deceased but that You show them mercy, and no need from the needs of this world or the Hereafter — in which is Your pleasure and our good — but that You fulfil it and make it easy, O Most Merciful of the merciful.
رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ.
Our Lord, grant us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and protect us from the punishment of the Fire.
al-Baqarah 2:201
سُبْحَانَ رَبِّكَ رَبِّ الْعِزَّةِ عَمَّا يَصِفُونَ، وَسَلَامٌ عَلَى الْمُرْسَلِينَ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ.

وَصَلَّى اللهُ وَسَلَّمَ وَبَارَكَ عَلَى نَبِيِّنَا مُحَمَّدٍ وَعَلَى آلِهِ وَصَحْبِهِ أَجْمَعِينَ.
Glory be to your Lord, the Lord of Might, above what they describe; and peace be upon the Messengers; and all praise is for Allah, Lord of all the worlds. May Allah send blessings, peace, and favour upon our Prophet Muḥammad, his family, and all his companions.
